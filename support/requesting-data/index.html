Denmark Statistics

Some more details about applying to Denmark Statistics can be found on the official SDCA website.

On this page are some more personal experiences of applying for data from DST. As a non-Danish speaking researcher, this was quite difficult since much of the documentation and help is in Danish. Plus, there are slight differences between the English version and the Danish. Of course, the Danish version is the most accurate, but it is not obvious where and if there are differences.
